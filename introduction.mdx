## What is Academia Gema?

Academia Gema is a SaaS platform built for volleyball clubs. It replaces spreadsheets and ad-hoc tools with a single, role-aware web application that handles the full lifecycle of a student's membership — from enrollment and class scheduling through attendance tracking, payment validation, injury management, and recovery sessions.

The frontend is a React single-page application served over the web. It communicates with a REST API backend and a PostgreSQL database. Three distinct user roles each get a purpose-built dashboard with their own layout, navigation, and permissions.

## User roles

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Administrador" icon="shield-halved" href="/roles/admin">
    Full access to all management features. Creates and manages students, teachers, schedules, locations, levels, payments, publications, benefits, guest passes, and system settings. Views analytics and exports Excel reports.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Coordinador / Profesor" icon="chalkboard-user" href="/roles/coordinator">
    Records daily attendance for their assigned classes, manages their personal profile, and processes cut-day summaries. Accesses a minimal sidebar focused on operational tasks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Alumno" icon="user-graduate" href="/roles/student">
    Enrolls in classes, views their schedule and registrations, reports and tracks injuries, books recovery sessions, uploads payment vouchers, reads club news, and manages their personal profile.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Scheduling & Enrollment" icon="calendar-days" href="/features/scheduling">
    Administrators create class schedules tied to locations, levels, and teachers. Students self-enroll through their dashboard. The admin panel handles bulk rescheduling and mass revert operations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Attendance tracking" icon="clipboard-check" href="/features/attendance">
    Coordinators mark attendance per class session. Students can view their full attendance history and per-class statistics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment management" icon="credit-card" href="/features/payments">
    Students upload payment vouchers and track their payment history. Administrators validate or reject submitted payments and manage accounts receivable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Injury & recovery management" icon="kit-medical" href="/api-reference/recoveries/overview">
    Students report injuries with details. Administrators oversee all active injuries across the club. Students book recovery sessions that are tracked separately from regular classes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Publications & news" icon="newspaper" href="/features/publications">
    Administrators publish club news and announcements. Students receive and read publications through their news feed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Benefits & discounts" icon="tag" href="/api-reference/payments/overview">
    Administrators create benefit types and assign discounts to eligible students. Pending benefit approvals are tracked in a dedicated queue.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Guest passes" icon="id-card" href="/roles/admin">
    Administrators issue and manage guest passes that allow non-members to attend sessions temporarily.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Excel reports" icon="file-excel" href="/features/payments">
    Generate detailed Excel exports for student rosters, payment history, and operational summaries using the `xlsx` and `xlsx-js-style` libraries.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
