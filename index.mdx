## Welcome to Academia Gema

Academia Gema is a full-stack volleyball club management platform. It brings together scheduling, enrollment, attendance tracking, and payments into a single system — with dedicated dashboards for each role in your organization.

## Core features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Class scheduling" icon="calendar" href="/features/scheduling">
    Manage weekly class schedules across multiple locations and courts, with coordinator assignments and training levels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Student enrollment" icon="user-plus" href="/features/enrollment">
    Enroll students into class schedules, manage active/suspended/withdrawn states, and cap class capacity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Attendance tracking" icon="clipboard-check" href="/features/attendance">
    Record present, absent, late, or excused attendance for each session per student enrollment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments & billing" icon="credit-card" href="/features/payments">
    Track accounts receivable, validate reported payments, apply discounts and benefits, and export reports.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Publications & news" icon="newspaper" href="/features/publications">
    Post club announcements and news for students to read in their dashboard.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recovery sessions" icon="rotate" href="/api-reference/recoveries/overview">
    Allow students to recover missed classes through alternative sessions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## User roles

Academia Gema has three distinct roles, each with its own dashboard and feature set.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Administrator" icon="shield-halved" href="/roles/admin">
    Full access to manage students, coordinators, schedules, payments, locations, levels, and system settings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Coordinator" icon="chalkboard-user" href="/roles/coordinator">
    Takes attendance for assigned classes and manages cut-day (dia de corte) records.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Student" icon="person" href="/roles/student">
    Views schedule, manages enrollment, tracks payments, reports injuries, and reads club news.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
